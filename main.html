LYRICAL

Every word of a song counts.

2019 - The Idea

So pretty much I wanted to find out how many common words does the English song FAITH by MADKID have. So as a result, I implemented a word counter for songs in Java through the use of a Hashmap. That way I can finally know how certain words come up.

Implementation

Hashmaps make it easy to know where to locate values based on keys and it allows for easy insertion/deletion, despite the lack of order. Figured, I don't need any need of sorting, just word frequency. So HashMaps make it one less problem for me. It also helps when a data is identified with a key pair, which in my case is just the frequency. Cool right?!

In the future I might add a GUI interface and make it look good for the average person. I could implement APIs that would call automatic extraction of lyrics based on a user's input of a song and they can search for it in a database. As for the website, add Frameworks and more Javascript to make it even more impressive!

The Song

FAITH is pretty much the second theme song to the second opening of The Rising of the Shield Hero anime I watched a while back. So I figured why not? It was really catchy and a good and inspirational beat with their other song RISE. The song was a hit in Japan and the anime is a hit from a few months back. Some words like "faith", "never", and "silent" really weren't that suprising (I do listen to this song 100 times a day after all). But it was crazy to see the words, "millions", "hundreds", and "change"pop up the most. These guys really love numbers. But it does make sense about a song about sheer determination. One of their chorus has this verse, "In the distorted world, following the footsteps, suppress the silent shout, hundreds of times, even,millions of times, I find & Create myself, now or never." See? These guys don't give up.
