LYRICAL

Every word of a song counts.

2019 - The Idea

So pretty much I wanted to find out how many common words does the English song FAITH by MADKID have. So as a result, I implemented a word counter for songs in Java through the use of a Hashmap. That way I can finally know how certain words come up.

Implementation

Hashmaps make it easy to know where to find key values to find the location of those lyrics. It makes a great search. Cool right?!

The Song

FAITH is pretty much the second theme song to the second opening of The Rising of the Shield Hero anime I watched a while back. So I figured why not?
